About

Dr. Jaime Denison is a scholar at the nexus of digital humanities and philosophy, dedicated to exploring how computational methods can enhance our understanding of human culture, thought, and experience.

Their research focuses on the application of digital tools to philosophical texts, the ethics of artificial intelligence, and the development of innovative pedagogical approaches that bridge traditional humanities education with modern technology.

Areas of Specialization

  • Nineteenth- and Twentieth-Century Continental Philosophy (German Idealism, Hegelianism, Psychoanalysis, Existentialism, Biopolitics)
  • Play Theory
  • Social and Political Philosophy
  • Critical Theory and Cultural Theory

Areas of Competency
